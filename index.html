About Lucas Lagoons

Where Stone, Water & Imagination Become Paradise

A big hello from Lucas and the crew. You may know us from our hit show Insane Pools: Off The Deep End — but our story started back in 2003, when Lucas Congdon combined his love of stone masonry and landscaping into something no one else was building: the custom lagoon.

Since then we've turned hundreds of ordinary backyards into one-of-a-kind paradises. We're priced at the top of the market for one reason — we build it right: over-engineered shells, premium finishes, and full-service design very few builders can match.

From one untapped idea in 2003 to four seasons on national TV, Lucas turned the "standard" pool on its head — and the original lagoon artist was born.

We think from a higher-end perspective. Every shell is built stronger and over-engineered to withstand years of weathering and shifting, so your paradise lasts a lifetime.

We're a true full-service builder. Landscaping, lighting, sound, waterslides and grottos — designed and built in-house, never cut-rate or outsourced.

  • Over-engineered, built-to-last pool shells
  • Full-service design, build & landscaping
  • Award-winning & nationally televised
  • Premium finishes tailored to your budget
